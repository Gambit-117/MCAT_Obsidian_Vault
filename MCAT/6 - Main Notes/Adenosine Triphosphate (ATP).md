# Adenosine Triphosphate (ATP)

ATP is the native energy source of the cell, created in mitochondria. 

As a neurotransmitter, ATP can carry signals between nerve cells in the central and peripheral nervous system. ATP is released in response to neuronal activity and other simuli.
