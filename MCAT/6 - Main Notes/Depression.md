# Depression

Depression is a common mental health condition characterized by symptoms of persistent low mood or sadness, loss of interest or pleasure in activities, changes in appetite or weight, sleep disturbances, and fatigue.

Depression is found in major depressive disorder, bipolar disorders, and dysthymia.
