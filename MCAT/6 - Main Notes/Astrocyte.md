# Astrocyte

Astrocytes are star-shaped cells that support the structure and function of the CNS. They are a type of glial cell, that help maintain chemical balances, the blood brain barrier, and the construction of synaptic connections. 

They are also able to secrete substances that influence the construction of new synaptic connections.
