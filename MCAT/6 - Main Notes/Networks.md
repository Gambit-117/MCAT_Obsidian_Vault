# Networks

A network is a group of interconnected people around you. A social network is a social structure between actors, connecting them through various social familiarities.

Social network theory is the study of how people, organizations, or groups interact with others inside their network.

An acquaintance within a social network is a person that one might know slightly, but who is not a close friend.

A clique is a small social group where all individuals know each other, and stay with each other exclusively.
