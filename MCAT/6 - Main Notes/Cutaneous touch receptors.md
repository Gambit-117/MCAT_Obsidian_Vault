# Cutaneous touch receptors

Cutaneous touch receptors are found in the dermis and the epidermis of our skin. There are several types of touch receptors, including Pacinian corpuscles, Merkel cells, Meissner corpuscles, Merkel's discs, and thermoreceptors and nociceptors.
