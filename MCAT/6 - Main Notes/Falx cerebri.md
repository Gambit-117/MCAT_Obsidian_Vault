# Falx cerebri

The falx cerebri is one of the major dural folds in the dura mater, along with the tentorium cerebelli. It attaches to the inner surface of the skull and the cerebral hemispheres, and it separates the two hemispheres of the brain. It is located in the longitudinal cerebral fissure between the hemispheres.
