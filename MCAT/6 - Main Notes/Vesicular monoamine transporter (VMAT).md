# Vesicular monoamine transporter (VMAT)

VMAT's are proteins located on the membranes of synaptic vesicles in neurons and neuroendocrine cells. Their primary function is to transport monoamine neurotransmitters, such as dopamine, serotonin, norepi, epi, and histamine from the cytoplasm of the neuron into vesicles.
