# Falx cerebelli

The falx cerebelli is one of the minor dural infoldings of the dura mater. It is a vertical dural infolding that lies inferior to the cerebellar tentorium in the posterior part of the posterior cranial fossa. It partial separates the cerebellar hemispheres.

It projects between the cerebral hemisphere and the inner occipital bone.
