# Conversion disorder

Conversion disorder is a psychiatric conditions characterized by physical symptoms that cannot be explained by any underlying mental condition. These symptoms are typically caused by psychological factors, such as stress, trauma, or conflict.

Common symptoms may include motor symptoms like paralysis, weakness, tremors, seizures, sensory symptoms like blindness, deafness, numbness and tingling, and cognitive symptoms such as difficulty speaking, swallowing, or walking.
