# Superior sagittal sinus

The superior sagittal sinus is an unpaired dural venous sinus lying along the attached margin of the falx cerebri. It allows blood to drain from the lateral aspects of the anterior cerebral hemispheres to the confluence of sinuses.

It is triangular in section; more narrow anteriorly and gradually increasing in size as it passes posterior-ward.
