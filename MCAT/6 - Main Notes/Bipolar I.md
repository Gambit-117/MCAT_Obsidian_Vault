# Bipolar I

Bipolar I is a chronic mental health condition characterized by extreme mood swings between manic and depressive episodes. Bipolar I is the only bipolar related disorders where swings into full mania occur. 

Symptoms of mania include elevated mood or euphoria, increased energy and activity levels, decreased need for sleep, and grandiosity. Symptoms of depression include persistent low mood or sadness, loss of interest or pleasure in activities, changes in appetite or weight, sleep disturbances, and fatigue.
