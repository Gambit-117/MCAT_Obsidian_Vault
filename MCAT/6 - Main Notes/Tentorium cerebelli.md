# Tentorium cerebelli

The tentorium cerebelli is one of the major dural reflections in the dura mater, alongside the falx cerebri. The tentorium cerebelli exists between and separates the cerebellum and brainstem from the occipital lobes of the cerebrum. It lies perpendicular to the falx cerebri.
