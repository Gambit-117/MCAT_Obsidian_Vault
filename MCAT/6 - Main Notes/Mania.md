# Mania

Mania is a state of abnormally elevated mood, energy, and activity that lasts for at least one week. It is a core symptom of bipolar disorder. 

Symptoms of mania include elevated mood or euphoria, increased energy and activity levels, decreased need for sleep, grandiosity, and increased reckless behavior.
