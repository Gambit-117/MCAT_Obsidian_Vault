# Potentiation

Potentiation is the enhanced ability of calcium to trigger vesicle fusion. Potentiation is the rise and fall of ability over a timescale of tens of seconds to minutes, induced by activated kinases that go on to phosphorylate substrates such as synapsin that regulate transmitter release.
