# basal nuclei

The basal nuclei, also known as the basal ganglia, is a group of interconnected structures located deep within the brain. They play a vital role in regulating voluntary motor control, learning, and behavior. Broadly categorized, it consists of input nuclei, output nuclei, and intrinsic nuclei.
