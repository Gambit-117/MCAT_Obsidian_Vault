# Sphincter of Oddi

The sphincter of Oddi is a muscular valve that controls the flow of pancreatic juice and bile into the duodenum. it is located in the wall of the duodenum where the common bile duct and the main pancreatic duct (duct of Wirsung) combine to enter the duodenum.
