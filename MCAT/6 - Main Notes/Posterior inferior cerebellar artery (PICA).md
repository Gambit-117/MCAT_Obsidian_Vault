# Posterior inferior cerebellar artery (PICA)

PICA is the largest branch of the vertebral artery. It is one of the three main arteries that supply blood to the cerebellum, a part of the brain. It is all the way at the posterior end of the vertebral artery, beneath the circle of Willis.
