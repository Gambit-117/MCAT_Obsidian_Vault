# Cataclysmic stressors
Cataclysmic stressors are stressors that result from a large tragedy affecting numerous peoples. They are usually related to large natural disasters, or extremely shocking large acts of violence.

As with any form of stressor, this may cause the activation of cortisol and epi/norepi.
