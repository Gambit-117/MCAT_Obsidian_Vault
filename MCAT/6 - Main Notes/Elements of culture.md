# Elements of culture

A culture is built up of many different types of elements, including but not limited to:

- Symbols - anything that carries a particular meaning recognized by people who share the same culture
- Language - a system of symbols that allows people to communicate with one another
- Values - Culturally-defined standards that serve as broad guidelines for social living
- Beliefs - Specific statements that people hold to be true
- Norms - rules and expectations by which a society guides the behavior of its members
