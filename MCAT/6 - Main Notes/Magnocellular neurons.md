# Magnocellular neurons

Magnocellular neurons are neurons that process visual information and regulate hormones. Several magnocellular neurons are located in the hypothalamus, supraoptic nuclei, and the paraventricular nuclei. They function to synthesize and secrete hormones like oxytocin and vasopressin.
