# Friction

Friction is the force that resists motion. Two forces of friction are important to note:

Static friction
$$ F_{SF} < \mu_{k}F_{N} $$
This force has a direct correlation to the principle of static equilibrium.

Kinetic friction 
$$ F_{KF} = \mu_{k}F_{N} $$
