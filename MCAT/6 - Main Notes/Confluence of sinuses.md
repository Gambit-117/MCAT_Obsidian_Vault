# Confluence of sinuses

The confluence of sinuses, also known as the torcular Herophili, is a point where several venous sinuses meet and drain into the transverse sinuses. it is located in the posterior fossa of the skull, inferior to the occipital lobes and superior to the cerebellum.

It includes the converging of the superior sagittal sinus, the straight sinus, the occipital sinus, and the two transverse sinuses.
