# General Adaptation Syndrome
General adaptation syndrome is a concept that describes three stages to general stress response: the alarm stage where "fight or flight" is triggered, the resistance stage where the body endures the stressor, and the exhaustion phase if the stressor persists and depletes the body's resources.
